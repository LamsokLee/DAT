{% include 'tab.html' %}

THIS TOOL (DECISION AID) IS FOR YOU IF:


  • You are the one who makes decisions for your loved one when they can’t do it for themselve
  • Your family member has a traumatic brain injury (TBI) and needs medical care in the ICU.
  • Your loved one needs machines to help them breathe and get food.

IN THIS DECISION AID, YOU WILL LEARN ABOUT:


  • The breathing machine (mechanical ventilator) and what it feels like to be on it
  • An outlook of the possible long-term effects from TBI in many other patients similar to your loved one
  • What is most important to your loved one and you
  • The choices you can make for your loved one for the goals of care decision
    • Facts about each choice
    • Advantages and disadvantages of each choice
    • Include your loved one’s values and preferences
  • The decision you are being asked to make
  • How to make the best decision for your loved one
