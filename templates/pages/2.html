THIS DECISION AID IS FOR YOU IF:


  • You are the one designated to make decisions for your family member when he/she can’t make those decisions on his/her own.
  • Your family member has a traumatic brain injury (TBI) and needs medical care in the ICU.
  • Your family member is on a machine to help with breathing and is fed through a tube (life support).

IN THIS DECISION AID YOU WILL LEARN ABOUT:


  • Breathing machines (ventilators) and what it feels like to use one;
  • Possible long-term effects of TBI (prognosis);
  • Goals of care decisions;
  • Identification of values most important to your family member and you;
  • Choices available when making the goals of care decision:
    • Facts about each choice;
    • Advantages and disadvantages of each choice;
    • Alignment of choices with your family member’s values and preferences;
  • How to make the best decision for your family member.
