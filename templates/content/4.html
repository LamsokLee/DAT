What decision do you have to make?

Your family member is still on a ventilator. Despite the best care from the doctors and nurses, he or she is unable to survive without the ventilator.

There are a number of reasons for this, for example:

  • Your family member is unconscious because of the brain injury, and the breathing tube is protecting the lungs from saliva or food going down the windpipe
  • Your family member has problems with the lungs themselves (lung infection, fluid on the lungs, or other condition)
  • Your family member has other issues, which the doctor can explain
