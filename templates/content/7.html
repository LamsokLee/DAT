How a doctor may reach a TBI prognosis

Note: This prognosis is based on your family member’s admission information for:
Age, how the pupils reacted to light, best motor response, oxygen levels at the accident scene or emergency room, blood pressure at the accident scene or emergency room, and head CT findings.

This prognosis does not include health before the brain injury, injuries to other organs and clinical course and complications in the ICU. Ask the doctor in the meeting how these may change your family member’s prognosis.
