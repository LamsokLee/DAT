COMPARING AND SUMMARIZING DIFFERENT TREATMENT OPTIONS


Reasons why families choose this option Reasons why families might avoid this option

“Do everything”



Advanced Medical Care focused on survival
  • It gives the patient the best chance of staying alive, even if just for a while.
  • Decision makers and family believe that the patient will have a quality of life acceptable to the patient if he/she survives
  • Additional treatments can be painful, or make the patient uncomfortable
  • The patient has told them that they do not wish to be dependent on others to live.
  • They know that the patient does not want a trach or feeding tube, even if just temporarily
  • The patient may still die, even with advanced treatments and suffer along the way

“No suffering”



Medical care founsed on comfort
  • It is unlikely that the patient will have a quality of life acceptable to the patient
  • They believe that the patient has suffered enough, and they want them to be comfortable and at peace
  • Without life supporting treatments, the patient will pass away
  • The patient would choose tubes, procedures and going to a nursing home if they were able to speak for themselves
