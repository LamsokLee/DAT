HOW CERTAIN OR UNCERTAIN IS THE OUTLOOK FOR MY LOVED ONE?


It is important to know that every patient is different. It is very difficult to tell exactly how your loved one will do.

Based on the outcomes of over 8,000 other TBI patients, a formula was made. It is called “IMPACT-model”. With it doctors can try to compare some of the factors from your loved one to those from the 8,000 other TBI patients to give an estimation of the outlook. However, this model does not take every patient factor into account, leaving a large degree of uncertainty.

It is important that you ask your loved one’s doctor to provide you an estimation of the outlook. Keep in mind that there is lots of uncertainty. It may be helpful for you to hear a range.

Ask for a “best case” and “worst case scenario”.
