WHAT IS MY LOVED ONE’S OUTLOOK?
WHAT ARE THE POSSIBLE LONG-TERM EFFECTS FROM THE TBI?


In order to make a decision, many families want and need to know about their loved one’s outlook (“prognosis”) and the possible long-term effects from TBI.

There is a wide range of possible long-term effects after TBI. This depends on many factors, including:

  • Severity of the initial brain injury
  • Age of the person at the time of injury
  • How healthy they were before the brain injury
  • Baseline memory and thinking before the brain injury
  • Oxygen and blood pressure levels immediately after the brain injury before getting to the ICU, and in the ICU.
  • Certain types of blood collections in the brain
  • Other serious injuries to other organs (lung, belly, etc.)
  • Complications in the ICU, such as serious infections or brain swelling etc.
  • Other unknown factors.
