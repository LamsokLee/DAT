WHAT ARE DIFFERENT TREATMENT GOALS FOR MY LOVED ONE?

There are two different treatment goals that you will be asked to choose between.

One treatment goal is survival of your loved one, no matter what it takes.

The other treatment goal is maximizing comfort for your loved one, so that they do not suffer.


We will explain both options in more detail over the next few pages.
